Hour a Day (tentative title)

I had been thinking about this idea while in my Essentials of Entrepreneurship class and developed the idea a little more in my Entrepreneurship seminar. The idea is a hybrid of Apple's Screen Time and OneSec which both aim to increase resistance to addictive phone applications. Their solutions alone were not good enough in my opinion, but a merging of their solutions in addition to my own flavor would tremendously increase the resistance and successfully aid in combatting addiction.
